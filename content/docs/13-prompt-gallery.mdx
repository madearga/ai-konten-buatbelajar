    ### Galeri Hasil (Ilustrasi)

    Di bawah ini adalah ilustrasi konsept bagaimana prompt berbeda-tipe menghasilkan output. **Gambar dibuat dengan prompt dummy untuk ilustrasi — bukan render sesunggu.**

    #### 1. Basic Prompt vs Detailed Prompt

    **Basic Prompt:**
    ```text
    glasses on gray background, rotating slowly
    ```

    **Detailed Prompt:**
    ```text
    A sleek pair of futuristic glasses, made of dark metal with gunmetal finish, 
    rotates slowly on a neutral gray studio background. Premium commercial aesthetic. 
    Camera starts with a wide shot (45-degree angle) then gradually zooms into a close-up of the frame. 
    Soft studio lighting from the left creates subtle reflections on the metal surface. 
    The glasses complete one full rotation over 8 seconds.
    ```

    **Perbedaan Kualitas (Ilustrasi):**
    - **Basic:** Hasil "standar" — wajah sederhana, lighting standar, tidak ada detail materials.
    - **Detailed:** Hasil "premium" — ada materials (gunmetal finish, reflections), lighting setup (studio + soft shadows), detail motion (slow rotation, zoom).

    #### 2. Contoh Product: Skincare Serum

    **Basic Prompt (Cepat):**
    ```text
    White skincare serum bottle, marble surface, natural light
    ```

    **Detailed Prompt (Presisi):**
    ```text
    Professional product photography of a white skincare serum bottle, 
    placed on a smooth polished marble surface with soft water droplets around the base. 
    Morning golden hour light filters through sheer curtains from the left, creating a warm, inviting atmosphere. 
    Subtle glow effect on the bottle surface, highlighting the product's premium positioning. 
    Shot on Phase One IQ4, 120mm f/2.8 lens, hyper-realistic, ultra detailed, 8K resolution. 
    Beauty editorial aesthetic, clean and minimal composition. 
    Shallow depth of field, bokeh background in soft-focus.
    ```

    **Contoh Gambar (Placeholder):**
    *   Basic: Botol putih dengan outline sederhana
    *   Detailed: Botol putih dengan water droplets, shadow halus, label premium yang jelas

    #### 3. Contoh Lifestyle: Supplement Bottle

    **Basic Prompt:**
    ```text
    Supplement bottle, wood counter
    ```

    **Detailed Prompt (Cerita):**
    ```text
    Lifestyle product photography of a supplement bottle, sitting on a rustic wooden kitchen counter next to a glass of water with fresh fruits visible in soft-focus background. 
    Natural morning light from a large window illuminates the scene with warm tones. 
    The label is clearly readable and facing camera, positioned naturally on the bottle's front. 
    Shot on Canon R5, 85mm f/1.4, shallow depth of field, warm color grading, realistic, aspirational, clean composition. 
    ```

    **Perbedaan:**
    - **Basic:** Hanya produk tanpa konteks. Bisa dipakai dimana saja.
    - **Detailed:** Ada cerita (morning di dapur), konteks (dapur kayu), mood (warm, healthy). Lebih emosional.

    #### 4. Contoh Animation: Liquid Metal Formation

    **Basic Prompt (Hanya Deskripsi):**
    ```text
    Glasses formed from liquid metal, transparent background
    ```

    **Detailed Prompt (Teknis):**
    ```text
    A sleek pair of futuristic glasses, constructed from liquid gunmetal alloy. 
    The temples gradually form from droplets that stretch into complete frames, creating a smooth metallic transition. 
    I used a Boolean operator to merge two geometries, so the temple structures exist seamlessly. 
    For the animation timing, I started with a 0.3-second ease-out and accelerated to a 0.8-second ease-in over 1.5 seconds. 
    The metal material has a polished surface with slight specular highlights that respond realistically to the environment's light.
    ```

    **Perbedaan:**
    - **Basic:** Akan terlihat sebagai "botol metal statis" yang tidak bergerak.
    - **Detailed:** Tempel dan droplets "terbentuk" dengan ease-in/out timing. Akan terlihat fluid dan organik.

    **Contoh Gambar (Placeholder):**

    *   Detailed: Animasi GIF (jika bisa) menunjukkan droplets merging

## Cara Hasilkan Prompt di Produsi

Untuk hasil yang sebenarnya di studio production (bukan ilustrasi), ikuti pipeline ini:

<Steps>

### Step 1: Pilih Prompt Template

Sesuaikan template prompt di Prompt Library dengan kebutuhan project:

- **Skincare** → Pakai template "Skincare Serum Product Shot"
- **Supplement** → Pakai template "Supplement Bottle Lifestyle"
- **Lipstick** → Pakai template "Lipstick Swatch Grid"
- **Snack/F&B** → Sesuaikan untuk lifestyle content

### Step 2: Generate Base Image (Nano Banana Pro)

Pilih model: **Nano Banana Pro 2** atau alternatif (Dreamina, Pixazo).

Prompt contoh (Detailed):
```text
Professional product photography of a [PRODUK] bottle, 
placed on a smooth marble surface with soft lighting and subtle product reflections.
Shot on Phase One IQ4, 120mm f/2.8, hyper-realistic, ultra detailed, 8K resolution.
Style: Beauty editorial, clean, minimal, luxury brand aesthetic.
```

### Step 3: Test & Iterate

Generate 3-5 variasi dengan sedikit perubahan prompt (warna lighting, angle berbeda). Pilih yang terbaik.

### Step 4: Video Generation (Seedance 2.0 / Sora 2)

Upload gambar terpilih ke **Seedance 2.0**:
- Choose: `Image to Video` → 10 detik video, cinematic motion
- Tambahkan: `text prompt` untuk kontrol kamera (pan, zoom, tilt)

Prompt video contoh:
```text
[Camera: Slow zoom in from wide shot to product close-up. Focus on the bottle's label and reflections.
[Motion: The camera orbits slowly around the product, creating a dynamic reveal that emphasizes its premium positioning.
[Timing: 5 seconds. In 0-2s: slow zoom reveal, 2-8s: orbit around product, 5s: final close-up holding position.
[Style: Luxury beauty product commercial, soft ambient lighting, shallow depth of field, high-end feel.
```

### Step 5: Lip Sync & Audio (ElevenLabs + Suno)

- Voice over: Generate dengan **ElevenLabs** (Indonesian voice untuk FMCG market)
- Music: Background track dari **Suno** atau library free

### Step 6: Final Edit (CapCut)

- Gabungkan semua clips
- Tambahkan subtitle (otomatis via CapCut atau ElevenLabs auto-caption)
- Branding overlay (logo, watermark)

</Steps>

## Prompt Testing Framework

Untuk membandingan hasil prompt, gunakan matrix ini:

| Dimensi | Basic | Detailed | JSON | Tips |
|---------|-------|----------|------|-----|
| **Kejelasan** | Rendah | Tinggi | Sangat Tinggi | Paling Jelas | ⚠️ Terlalu kompleks |
| **Waktu** | Cepat | Sedang | Lambat | Sangat Lambat | ⚠️ Inefisien |
| **Konsistensi** | Rendah (30%) | Sedang (50%) | Tinggi (70%) | Sangat Tinggi (90%) | ⚠️ Tidak repeatabil |
| **Kemungkinan Variasi** | Tinggi (random) | Menengah | Sedang | Rendah (low) | Paling Rendah (random) | ⚠️ Sulit prediksi |
| **Kesalahan Error** | Sering (salah kata, typo JSON) | Jarang | Jarang | Sangat Jarang | Hampir Nol | ⚠️ Paling Aman |

## Catatan untuk Pemula

<Tabs items={['Mulai Pakai AI', 'Common Pitfalls', 'Workflow Best Practices']}>
  <Tab>
    ### Mulai Pakai AI

    **1. Mulai dengan Basic Prompt**
    Tambahkan detail sedikit demi menigkatkan kualitas:
    - `glasses on gray background` → `sleek metal glasses on gray background, polished surface`
    - `serum bottle` → `professional serum bottle on marble surface with studio lighting`

    **2. Gunakan Seedance "Smart Refine"**
    Setelah generate, Seedance auto-offers 2-3 rekomendasi. Pilih yang paling mirip prompt kamu.

    **3. Test Sebelum Produksi**
    Gunakan free tier dulu (Dreamina, Kling) untuk cek apakah prompt kamu bekerja.

  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Common Pitfalls

    **⚠️ Randomness**
    Jangan berharap hasil sama setiap kali. Seed matters!

    **⚠️ Over-Engineering**
    Prompt tidak harus lebih panjang = lebih baik. Detail yang tidak relevan hanya bikin AI bingung.

    **⚠️ Format Errors**
    JSON error: satu kurung bracket buka = seluruh prompt gagal.
    Selalu validate JSON di tool seperti `jsonlint.com`.

    **⚠️ Vague Descriptions**
    "Good lighting" → "Soft studio lighting from upper left with key light at 1.2m distance, fill 30% with soft fill"
    Lebih spesifik = lebih mungkin AI mengerti apa yang kamu mau.

  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Workflow Best Practices

    **Version Control**
    Simpan setiap iterasi prompt di Notion atau file terpisah:
    ```
    [Project: Skincare Serum]
    V1.0: Basic prompt
    V1.1: Added materials (gunmetal finish)
    V1.2: Detailed prompt — WINNER
    ```

    **A/B Testing**
    Produksi 2-3 variasi dengan prompt berbeda:
    - Versi A: Basic prompt
    - Versi B: Detailed prompt (lebih mahal)
    A/B test di klien → pilih yang lebih baik.

    **Document Hasil**
    Catat apa yang berhasil dan apa yang gagal:
    - Prompt jenis apa yang paling bekerja untuk brand kamu?
    - Model AI apa yang paling andal?

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
