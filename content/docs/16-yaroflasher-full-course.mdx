## Lesson 1 — FlashBoards & Nano Banana

### Workflow Dasar: Fashion Photo Shoot

**Setup:** 16:9, 1K resolution (iterasi cepat dulu)

**Prompt:**
```text
Create a fashion photo shoot
```

**Input:** 2 images — close-up wajah + full body shot (supaya face + outfit konsisten)

**Tips Yaroflasher:**
- Generate **3 outputs** sekaligus — "One is gambling, three is strategy"
- Pilih winner → baru upscale ke 2K/4K
- Double-click image → lihat info prompt + model yang dipakai
- Select multiple → double-click → flip seperti storyboard

---

### Workflow dengan Pinterest Reference

**Prompt:**
```text
Replace the woman in the second image with the man in the first image
```

**Prompt versi artistic:**
```text
Make an artistic photo shoot similar to the second image
with the man from the first image
```

**Tips:** English tidak harus perfect — Nano Banana paham bahasa campuran. Bisa tulis pakai bahasa sendiri.

---

### Workflow: Compose to Collage (Reference Board)

Kumpulkan multiple references dari Pinterest (pose, lighting, angle, outfit) → gabung jadi satu board → feed ke Nano Banana.

**Keuntungan:** Satu image berisi multiple panels → lighting ideas, camera angles, poses, komposisi — semua dalam satu input.

---

### Upscale Pipeline

```
Generate di 1K → Pilih winner → Upscale ke 2K atau 4K
```

**Prompt upscale:**
```text
Ultra high quality
```

**Aturan penting:** Deselect semua dulu → select HANYA image yang mau di-upscale. Kalau masih ada image lain terseleksi, Nano Banana akan upscale yang salah.

**Perbandingan 1K vs 2K:**
- 1K: testing cepat (30 detik), detail cukup
- 2K: edges lebih tajam, texture lebih jelas, face detail lebih clean
- 4K: untuk final delivery ke klien

## Lesson 2 — Sora 2 Workflow

### Sora Storyboard Method

**Prompt:**
```text
Make fun realistic photo storyboard
```

**Prompt untuk video:**
```text
Man singing / man talking / man rapping
```

**Tips Sora:**
- Selalu upload **4K images** untuk hasil terbaik (tanpa jitter)
- Gunakan storyboard — "That's the only way I really use Sora, specifically with storyboards"
- Tambahkan **extreme close-up of the face** di storyboard untuk menjaga consistency
- Jika ada watermark → butuh subscription $200

---

### Sora Pro Workflow (Full HD)

**Setup:** Sora Pro, 15 detik → output langsung Full HD

**Prompt music video:**
```text
Man singing, cinematic urban night scene, neon reflections,
camera slowly orbiting, moody lighting, zip jacket half open
```

**Hasil:** Full HD langsung — tidak perlu upscale lagi. "You can literally take those clips, cut a final edit, and deliver to a client as is."

---

### Sora 25-Second Workflow

**Setup:** 25 detik, output HD (bukan Full HD)

**Note:** Quality lebih rendah dari 15-detik Pro, tapi durasi lebih panjang.

---

### Sora Cameo (Character Consistency)

1. Download **Sora mobile app**
2. Buat character tag (contoh: `@Yaroflasher`)
3. Set ke **public** (bukan private)
4. Include tag di prompt → character konsisten antar scenes

**Tips:** Setelah membuat Cameo, gunakan nama tag di setiap prompt Sora.

---

### Sora vs Kling 3 Comparison

| Aspek | Sora 2 | Kling 3 |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Multi-shot** | Lebih banyak, lebih sinematik | Terbatas |
| **Lip sync** | Cukup bagus | **Lebih baik** di close-up |
| **Character consistency** | Storyboard + Cameo | Face preservation feature |
| **Watermark** | Ada (perlu $200 sub) | Tidak ada |
| **Omni mode** | Tidak ada | Ada — bagus untuk transformation |

**Kesimpulan Yaroflasher:** "Sora 2 is still better than Kling 3. If you're planning to do very high quality lip sync, I'd recommend Kling. But for everything else, Sora performs better."

## Lesson 3 — ComfyUI Image Workflows

### Image Expand (Flux Klein)

**Kecepatan:** Under 10 detik (meskipun UI tunjukkan 55 detik)

**Model:** Flux Klein — tercepat untuk utility tasks (expand, cleanup, quick iterations)

---

### Camera Angle Workflow

**Search:** `camera` di FlashBoards workflows

**Keunggulan:** Presisi rotasi kamera yang Nano Banana tidak bisa handle.

**Contoh angles:**
- Back right quarter
- Right side view, low angle
- Full back view

**Note:** Nano Banana sering "miss" — bukan rotasi kamera yang benar, lebih ke "vibe change". Pakai dedicated camera workflow untuk hasil akurat.

---

### Wash Out (Reference Legal Protection)

**Fungsi:** Keep the concept, remove the legal headache

**Intensity levels:**
- **0.3** — Light wash, mostly enhancement. Details cleaner, lines nicer, still close to original. Gunakan untuk enhance image sendiri.
- **0.6** — Patterns shift, clothing changes, objects update. Core idea tetap sama. Gunakan untuk safe reference-based image.
- **0.9** — Full transformation. Same concept, completely new execution. "At 0.9, you're already safe to use it."

---

### Change Pose (Pose Transfer)

**Winner:** Nano Banana — "No debate"

**Workflow:**
1. Pilih source image
2. Pilih reference image (pose yang diinginkan)
3. Tulis prompt → Done

**Note:** Free ComfyUI pose workflow ada, tapi di cloud bisa lebih mahal karena GPU time lebih lama daripada Nano Banana.

---

### Outfit Change

**Winner:** Nano Banana untuk clean production-ready result

**Prompt:**
```text
Take the outfit from the second image and apply it to the first
```

**Free workflow advantage:** No restrictions — bisa push lebih keras, tapi hasil kurang konsisten (kadang jadi "dog head on human body").

---

### Upscale vs Restoration — Perbedaan Penting

<Callout type="warning" title="Jangan Campur Adukkan!">
**Restoration** ≠ **Upscale** — dua proses berbeda!

- **Restoration** = Perbaiki image yang jelek (artifacts, noise, skin damage) → jadi normal lagi
- **Upscale** = Image sudah clean → buat lebih besar dan lebih tajam

**Urutan yang benar:** Restoration dulu → Upscale sesudah
</Callout>

**Upscale Nano Banana:**
```text
Ultra high quality
```

**Free Upscale (ComfyUI - Zimage Plus):**
- Kadang lebih bagus texture-nya daripada Nano Banana
- Tapi bisa mengubah face → solusi: face swap setelah upscale

**Topaz vs FlashBoards Restoration:**
| Aspek | Topaz | FlashBoards Restoration |
|-------|-------|------------------------|
| **Approach** | Faithful upscale — keep exactly the same | Smart repair + upgrade |
| **Face** | Preserves micro details | Slight reinterpretasi |
| **Harga** | Mahal | ~20x lebih murah |
| **Best for** | Commercial shot, exact expression | AI content, fast iterations, banyak variations |

---

### Realism Transfer

**Prompt:**
```text
Make realistic
```

**Note:** Kling-based workflows, ~10x lebih murah dari Nano Banana. Nano Banana juga bisa realism, kadang lebih expressive untuk environments.

---

### Character Replacement

**Winner:** Kling — "Very reliable, clean swaps, no drama"

---

### Budget Strategy (Ringkasan)

<Callout type="success" title="Manajemen Budget Yaroflasher">
"Manage your budget, not your emotions."

- **Free/ultra-cheap workflows** → prompt testing, mood boards, early drafts, style exploration, background changes, minor edits
- **Nano Banana** → final looks, upscale, polish, production-ready output

"If you try to do everything in Nano Banana, you're not being professional. You're just burning money. Real workflow = mix and match."
</Callout>

## Lesson 4 — ComfyUI Video Workflows

### Lip Sync Pipeline (Zero Budget Method)

**Pipeline:**
1. Generate video di Sora/Kling
2. Ambil **clean frame** dari video (di DaVinci Resolve, bukan dari original image)
3. **Upscale frame** dengan Nano Banana (1K atau HD cukup)
4. Feed ke **Google Veo** untuk lip-sync

**Prompt lip-sync:**
```text
[Describe mood, energy, emotion]
[paste exact spoken text]
```

**Kenapa ambil frame dari video?** "This guarantees perfect visual continuity. Same lighting, same angle, same character, same moment in time."

**Tips:** "If the lip sync needs adjustment, fix it in DaVinci — not in the model. Slight retiming, micro speed changes, easing with optical flow."

---

### Video Restoration vs Topaz

**Topaz Video Upscale:**
- Push ke 4K
- Lines cleaner, edges tighter, more structured
- Tapi perbedaan kadang subtle

**FlashBoards Video Restoration:**
- Restoration pass — repair + enhance, bukan cuma upscale
- Texture lebih natural, details lebih stable
- "The video got a proper cleanup and got its confidence back"

---

### AutoSplit + Quick Edit (FlashBoards)

1. **AutoSplit** → deteksi otomatis setiap cut/shot change
2. **Audisi seperti playlist** → keep/remove tiap bagian
3. **Merge** → gabung kembali jadi final clip

"Speedrunning an edit, but the result still looks professional."

## Lesson 5 — AI Music Video Practice

### Full Pipeline ($14.50 Budget)

**Total cost:** ~$14.50 untuk full stylized cinematic music video

**Pipeline:**

<Steps>

### Step 1: Generate Base Clips
Nano Banana → fashion photo shoot → upscale ke 4K

### Step 2: Sora Storyboard Video
Upload 4K images ke Sora → generate 15 detik per scene

### Step 3: Audio Separation
Music vocal extraction → pisahkan vocal dari music

### Step 4: Lip Sync
Grab frame dari video → upscale → feed ke Veo → lip-sync

### Step 5: Edit di DaVinci Resolve
Speed ramp 500-800% → sembunyikan glitch + bikin rhythm

### Step 6: Final Color Grade + Audio Mix
Polish warna, sync audio, export

</Steps>

---

### Acceleration Trick (500-800%)

**Prompt insight:** "Accelerating AI video by 500-800% not only creates rhythm but also hides micro-glitches and melted pixels."

**Cara kerja:**
- Speed up video 5-8x lipat
- Glitch micro yang tadinya terlihat → hilang karena terlalu cepat
- Hasil: rhythm yang dinamis + visual yang terlihat lebih clean

## Lesson 6 — AI Anime Trailer

### Story Building dengan Pinterest

**Prompt:**
```text
Make man in first image riding motorbike as woman in second image
```

**Workflow:**
1. Cari reference di Pinterest (style slot machine — "You find one thing you like, Pinterest gives you 200 versions of the same vibe")
2. Copy image → paste ke Nano Banana
3. Select first image = style reference, second image = character reference
4. Generate 2-4 outputs ("If you generate only one, you're basically gambling")

---

### Character Development Pipeline

**Prompt untuk partner character:**
```text
Create a female character matching the style of @image 1,
sitting behind the man on the motorcycle
```

**Prompt untuk antagonist:**
```text
Create a girl with a machine gun in anime style,
matching the visual style of @image 1
```

**Story template:** Hero saves girl → villain attacks → action sequence

---

### Anime Style Consistency

**Workflow:**
1. Lock style dan main look di 1K dulu
2. Setelah lock → bebas ubah camera, lighting, environment, outfit, hairstyle
3. "Find a reference, steal the vibe, repeat"

**Tips:** Generate Nano Banana images di 1K dulu (15-30 detik), lalu upscale winner ke 2K/4K.

---

### Camera Experimentation

**Prompt untuk closer shots:**
```text
Extreme close-up of the character's face,
dramatic anime lighting, same style as @image 1
```

**Prompt untuk action scene:**
```text
Wide shot of motorcycle chase scene,
explosions in background, anime action style
```
