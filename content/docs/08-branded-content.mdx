# Branded Content Campaign

## UGC vs Branded Content

| | UGC Ads | Branded Content |
|---|---------|-----------------|
| **Style** | Casual, "dibuat user" | Premium, on-brand |
| **Tujuan** | Performance (klik, beli) | Awareness, positioning |
| **Durasi** | 15-30 detik | 30-90 detik |
| **Format** | Vertical (9:16) | Multi-format |
| **Budget production** | $2-10/video | $50-200/video |

Branded content untuk **FMCG/Beauty/Health** biasanya berupa:
- Product launch video
- Brand story / manifesto
- Seasonal campaign (Ramadan, Natal, Anniversary)
- Social media campaign assets

![Contoh branded content untuk brand health supplement — influencer fitness Indonesia memegang protein shake di gym, gaya UGC yang believable](/images/examples/arena-protein-shake-fitness.png)

<Callout type="tip" title="Branded Content Health Supplement">
Gambar di atas dihasilkan dari prompt "Fitness influencer holding a chocolate protein shake shaker bottle in gym, mid-workout sweat glow, athletic Indonesian man in compression shirt" — menggunakan model **gpt-image-2** di arena.ai.

Branded content untuk FMCG/Health: fokus pada cerita dan emotional connection, bukan hard sell. Coba beberapa model untuk lihat mana yang paling cocok dengan brand voice.
</Callout>

## Campaign Workflow

<Steps>

### Creative Brief
Sebelum mulai, siapkan:

| Element | Contoh |
|---------|--------|
| **Brand** | "GlowSkin" (skincare lokal) |
| **Campaign** | "Ramadan Glow" |
| **Target** | Wanita 20-35, urban, pede issue |
| **Key message** | "Kulit glowing mespu puasa" |
| **Deliverables** | 1 hero video (30s) + 5 social posts |
| **Platform** | TikTok + Instagram + YouTube Shorts |

### Visual Direction
Generate moodboard di Dreamina atau Midjourney:
```
Minimal beauty moodboard, soft pastel palette,
natural textures, clean typography,
luxury skincare aesthetic, warm tones,
Indonesian beauty, golden hour lighting
```

### Produksi Assets
<Tabs items={['Hero Video', 'Social Posts']}>
  <Tab>
    ### Hero Video (30-60 detik)

    1. **Storyboard** — 6-8 frames, tulis prompt per frame
    2. **Generate frames** — Nano Banana Pro per shot
    3. **Image-to-video** — Seedance 2.0 (UGC feel) atau Sora 2 (premium)
    4. **Audio** — ElevenLabs voice over + Suno background music
    5. **Edit** — CapCut, gabungkan semua elements

    **Script hero video (Ramadan campaign):**
    ```
    [0-5s]  Woman wakes up, sahur preparation, soft morning light
    [5-15s] Skincare routine — applies serum, gentle motions
    [15-25s] Throughout the day — glowing, confident, at work
    [25-35s] Iftar moment — breaks fast, skin still radiant
    [35-45s] Product hero shot — bottle center frame, warm tones
    [45-50s] CTA: "Ramadan Glow — tersedia di [retailer]"
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab>
    ### Social Posts (5 variations)

    1. **Carousel** — Before/after skincare routine
    2. **Reel** — 15 detik quick routine timelapse
    3. **Story** — Poll "Skincare routine kamu saat puasa?"
    4. **Static post** — Product shot + Ramadan greeting
    5. **UGC-style video** — AI character testimonial

    Setiap post: generate image/video → add text overlay di Canva → export
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Multi-Platform Adaptation

| Source | Adaptasi | Tools |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Hero video (16:9) | Reel (9:16) | CapCut auto-reframe |
| Hero video | 15s highlight | Manual cut |
| Product shots | Carousel post | Canva batch resize |
| Storyboard frames | Static posts | Direct export |

### Schedule & Deploy
- **Timing:** Test posting waktu (10:00, 12:00, 19:00, 21:00 WIB)
- **Sequence:** Hero video dulu → social posts spread 3-5 hari
- **Monitor:** Engagement per platform → iterate

</Steps>
