## Workflow: Image-to-Video

Ini workflow yang paling umum dan paling bisa diandalkan:

<Steps>

### Siapkan Starting Image
Gunakan karakter AI dari Modul 04 (Nano Banana). Pilih gambar terbaik yang ingin "dihidupkan".

Pastikan:
- ✅ Karakter jelas terlihat
- ✅ Pose natural (tidak terlalu rigid)
- ✅ Lighting bagus
- ✅ Produk terlihat (jika ada)

### Tulis Video Prompt
Struktur video prompt:

```
[CAMERA] + [SUBJECT] + [ACTION] + [ENVIRONMENT] + [DURATION] + [MOOD]
```

**Contoh untuk skincare UGC:**
```
Static medium shot, young woman in bathroom,
gently applies serum to cheek with upward motions,
smiles naturally at camera,
soft morning light, warm tones,
8 seconds, beauty UGC lifestyle feel
```

### Generate Video
Masukkan image + prompt ke platform:
- **Kling** (free tier) — untuk testing
- **Seedance 2.0** — untuk UGC + lip sync
- **Sora 2** — untuk premium branded content

### Check Quality
Periksa hasil:
- ✅ Motion natural (tidak "uncanny")?
- ✅ Karakter tetap konsisten?
- ✅ Produk tetap terlihat?
- ✅ Durasi cukup?
- ✅ Tidak ada artefak visual?

### Iterate
Kalau motion kurang natural, ubah action verb:
- "gently moves" → "slowly turns"
- "walks toward camera" → "leans forward"
- Tambah specific: "left hand moves up, right hand holds bottle"

</Steps>

## Video Prompt Templates

```

pumps serum into palm, rubs hands together,
presses onto cheeks with gentle upward motions,
looks at camera and smiles,

8 seconds, beauty routine aesthetic
```

## Sora 2 — Deep Dive

**Sora 2** adalah yang terbaik untuk video premium. Cocok untuk:
- Brand film 30-60 detik
- Product launch video
- Storytelling campaign

### Prompt Tips Sora 2 (dari Yaroflasher)

```
[Camera movement], [subject description], [action],
[environment/setting], [lighting condition], [mood/style]
```

**Gunakan camera terminology:**
- `slow dolly in` — kamera maju pelan
- `tracking shot` — kamera mengikuti subject
- `static` — kamera diam
- `slight handheld` — goyangan natural
- `crane up` — kamera naik

**Contoh premium (Beauty brand):**
```
Slow dolly in, elegant woman in white silk robe,
walks through sunlit botanical garden,
holding skincare bottle, stops and turns to camera,
golden hour light filtering through leaves,
cinematic 35mm, warm color grading, luxury aesthetic
```

## Seedance 2.0 — Lip Sync

Keunggulan Seedance 2.0: **lip sync native** — karakter "berbicara" sesuai audio.

### Workflow Lip Sync

1. Buat karakter image (Modul 04)
2. Tulis script voice over (Modul 05)
3. Generate audio dengan **ElevenLabs** (Modul 03)
4. Upload image + audio ke Seedance 2.0
5. AI generate video dengan lip sync otomatis

Ini workflow yang **paling powerful untuk UGC ads** — karakter AI berbicara promosi produk.
